# Support

Need help with VoiceLocal? Reach out and we'll get back to you.

Contact us at [support@voicelocalapp.com](mailto:support@voicelocalapp.com).

## Quick fixes

- **Extension shows "not running"?** Make sure the VoiceLocal Mac app is open and running in your menu bar.
- **Transcription not working?** Check that VoiceLocal has Microphone and Accessibility permissions in macOS System Settings → Privacy & Security.
